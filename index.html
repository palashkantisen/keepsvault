About

KeepsVault makes it easy to keep important things in one place and share safely with whānau. It is simple to use, with clear controls for privacy and access. Our focus is trust, security, and plain language.

Help / FAQ

What is a “card”?

A card is one item. It can be a note, a file, a date, or a person. You can group cards into stacks.

How do I share?

Open a card or stack and choose who to share with. Pick view or edit.

Can others edit?

Only if you give edit access. You can change this any time.

Is my info safe?

We use strong security and privacy controls. Your data stays yours.
