Security

This page gives a plain view of how we protect your data.

Data protection

  • Encryption in transit (HTTPS/TLS) and at rest.
  • Backups with regular restore tests.
  • Least-privilege access for staff and systems.

Account security

  • Strong passwords recommended; MFA planned for app launch.
  • Session management with expiry.

Operations

  • Monitoring and alerting for core services.
  • Change control and review of key components.

Incidents

  • We will investigate and notify affected users where required by law.
